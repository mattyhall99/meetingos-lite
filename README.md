**Turn any meeting transcript into a quick summary with action items.**

MeetingOS Lite is a free Claude skill that processes meeting notes from any source and gives you two things:

1. A **TL;DR** — 2-3 sentences. The 10-second version of what happened.
2. An **Actions table** — every commitment, task, and follow-up with owners and due dates.

That's it. Fast, clean, useful. No configuration needed.

## Supported Sources

- **Google Meet / Gemini** — searches your Gmail and Drive automatically
- **Zoom, Otter, Fireflies** — upload the transcript or notes file
- **Microsoft Teams** — upload the meeting notes
- **Any transcript** — paste into chat or upload text / markdown / PDF / docx

## Installation

### Claude.ai (Web / App)

1. Download this repo (or clone it)
2. Copy the `.claude/skills/meetingos-lite` folder to your Claude skills directory:
   - macOS / Linux: `~/.claude/skills/meetingos-lite/`
   - Windows: `%USERPROFILE%\.claude\skills\meetingos-lite\`
3. Start a new Claude conversation

### Claude Code

```bash
# From your project directory
mkdir -p .claude/skills
cp -r path/to/meetingos-lite .claude/skills/meetingos-lite
```

Or add this repo as a skill source directly:

```bash
claude skills add https://github.com/mattyhall99/meetingos-lite
```

## Usage

Just say any of these:

- "Summarise that meeting"
- "What were the actions from yesterday's sync?"
- "Process my meeting notes"
- Upload a transcript and say "quick summary"

MeetingOS Lite will find the notes (searching Gmail and Drive if needed), extract the content, and present a summary inline.

## Example Output

Here's what MeetingOS Lite produces from a real 39-minute Scrum of Scrums transcript:

```
## Client Readiness Scrum of Scrums — 26 May 2026

**Attendees:** 12 participants (5 sent apologies)

### TL;DR
The team flagged R1 as Red after nine new uncommitted signals surfaced
requiring engineering support the team does not have capacity for.
Leadership responded with a clear escalation framework: assess feasibility,
articulate consequences, and surface resource shortfalls transparently.
The Doc Chase platform recovered to Green after firewall resolution.

### Actions
| Action                                                        | Owner         | Due            |
|---------------------------------------------------------------|---------------|----------------|
| Populate document with questions ahead of Thursday            | All leads     | Before Thu     |
| Submit all dependencies to finalise requirements              | Mark          | Fri 30 May     |
| Clarify trigger mechanism with engineering team               | David         | Thu 29 May     |
| Follow up on status for launch                                | Matt          | 26 May         |
| Determine feasibility and resource impact                     | John          | TBD (urgent)   |
| Split tracker: Original scope (green) vs remaining scope (red)| Matt          | 26 May         |
| Follow up on overdue commitment dates from 22 May             | Matt          | 26 May         |
| Demo basic agent                                              | Shane         | End of week    |

8 actions identified.

---
Generated by MeetingOS Lite. Upgrade to MeetingOS Pro for insights,
risks, blockers, decisions, and exec summaries.
```

Compare that to the AI transcript tool's own summary of the same meeting:

> "Teams reviewed product release milestones and addressed project status shifts alongside resource management for new signals."

The gap between those two outputs is why MeetingOS exists.
